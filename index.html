About me

I am a creative UX/UI Designer who is always eager to learn new things and skills. I have one year of experience working on projects, and my passion is designing modern responsive websites and mobile app. I have a deep understanding of app, web, and graphic design practices like color schemes, typography, consistency, user-friendliness, and more. I have experience working with teams and brainstroming. I can create wireframes and varying levels of fidelity, and enjoy prototyping ideas. I have front-end coding experience, and enjoy working with devs to build ideas out.
