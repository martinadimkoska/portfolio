Competetive Analysis

After learning more about the industry, I wanted to take a closer look at Gallery 94 ’s competitors and how they’re helping their users shop for art and equipment. Through my market research, I identified some top direct and indirect competitors within the industry: Artmajeur, Lisson Gallery, Saatchairt, Mihail Korubin. Direct Exploring each of their web-sites, I evaluated the strengths and weaknesses of each to see how Gallery 94 could fill in any gaps moving forward.

User Persona

To make sure that my decisions moving forward in the process are user-centered, I wanted to have a clear understanding of who Gallery94’s users are. Using what I learned from patterns from my empathy map, I created a user persona to represent who I will be designing for - Meet Blagoj and Goran!.
